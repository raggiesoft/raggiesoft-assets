**LORE NOTE:**
"The Masquerade" introduces the explicit concept of the "fragile fortress"—a direct reference to the physical and emotional sanctuary Rhys built for Claire in Williamsport. The "gallery of ghosts" represents the haunting presence of their four deceased bandmates. By dressing up and playing the parts of a full six-piece band using mechanical synthesizers and salvaged instruments, Rhys and Claire are putting on a theatrical "masquerade" to mask the overwhelming emptiness left behind.
