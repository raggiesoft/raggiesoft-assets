**LORE NOTE:**
"The Fading Spark" captures the profound, paranoid anxiety of maintaining the "fragile fortress" Rhys built for Claire in Maryland. The fortress of synthesizers and drum machines was engineered not just to make music, but to create a highly controlled, predictable environment to keep Claire's sensory processing safe from the overwhelming noise of the outside world, and to keep the ghosts of their family at bay. 

The track's frantic, staccato rhythm mimics a failing machine. When Claire plays the fast, echoing flute solo—sounding "like a bird trapped in a machine"—it represents the terrifying realization that their isolated mechanical sanctuary is only a temporary fix, and the emotional weight of their grief might eventually cause the whole system to crash.
