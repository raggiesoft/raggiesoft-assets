**LORE NOTE:**
A multi-part epic that musically depicts Leo's primary survival mechanism: the **"Painted Smile."**

**1. The Mask:**
The "Painted Smile" is defined as a "hollow mask of endurance." It is not a genuine expression of happiness; it is a rigid, forced facade of compliance that Leo locks into place to hide his fear from Delores. It serves as a survival shield, protecting his "broken parts" from a predator who feeds on his emotional reactions.

**2. The Sonic Shift:**
The song begins as a gentle 80s power ballad, representing his authentic, safe self when he is with his sisters. As Delores's presence looms over the "Sibling Weekend," the track transitions into a tense, driving rock bridge. Finally, in the closing moments, the music violently shatters into a **cold, heavy industrial sound**. This sonic shift mimics the dissociation Leo experiencesâ€”becoming mechanical and silent to endure the trauma.

**3. The Narrative Arc:**
This track marks the moment the trap snaps shut. It establishes the psychological barrier Leo will live behind for the entirety of Disc 1. The mask will not fall until the track "Sanctuary" on Disc 2, when he is finally safe on his native ground.
