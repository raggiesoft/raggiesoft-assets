**LORE NOTE:**
The album's emotional climax. The setting is the arrival gate at **Norfolk International Airport (ORF)**.

When Leo rolls off the jetway and sees Stella and Luna waiting for him, the overwhelming relief triggers the most severe autistic meltdown of his life. It is a "System Crash" born of the unbearable reality that he is actually safe.

**The Promise:**
The most critical moment of the entire rock opera occurs in the second verse. **Luna (Isabella Croft)** delivers the line that finally breaks the trauma loop: **"Your broken heart will mend with time."**

This isn't a platitude. It is a binding vow from sister to brother. It is Luna using her voice to overwrite the damage Delores did, promising him that his current pain is temporary, but his family is permanent. When she sings this line, the "Painted Smile" mask doesn't just fall; it shatters, and Leo finally allows himself to be held.
