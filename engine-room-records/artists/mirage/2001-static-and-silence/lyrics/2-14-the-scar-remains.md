**LORE NOTE:**
A mature, reflective rock track that serves as the thematic resolution of the album.

If "Sanctuary" was the relief of rescue, **"The Scar Remains"** is the hard work of recovery. Leo acknowledges that he cannot simply "reset" to the boy he was in Track 1 ("Nineteen and Invincible"). That version of him is gone, lost to the "Long Road West."

Instead of fighting this loss, he accepts it. The lyrics describe the slow, non-linear process of learning to trust againâ€”not just his family, but his own judgment.

**The Sonic Synthesis:**
Musically, this track represents the complete integration of the band's sound. It features **Luna's Flute** (representing her emotional promise) weaving seamlessly with **Stella's New Wave Synthesizer** (representing her structural protection), both grounded by a steady, classic 80s rock beat. It is the sound of his support system becoming part of his own internal rhythm.
