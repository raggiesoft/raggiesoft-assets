# The Dependency Loop

**LORE NOTE:**
This track delves into the specific mechanics of Aubrie Sinclair's control over her son. She deliberately weaponized "care" by refusing to teach Jordan basic life skills, such as how to dress himself or tie his shoes, forcing him to rely entirely on her for survival. By framing the outside world as dangerous and positioning herself as his sole protector and provider, she created a suffocating "cage of comfort." The song also touches upon her medical deception—keeping him unvaccinated and isolated from doctors—ensuring her absolute authority over his physical and mental well-being to guarantee he would never leave her.
