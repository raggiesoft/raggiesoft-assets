# Route Four-Sixty (The Seven-Hour Drive)

**LORE NOTE:**
This track captures the tense, seven-hour journey from the humid flatlands of Hampton Roads to the rolling mountains of the New River Valley for college move-in day. Traveling westward along U.S. Route 460, Jordan is trapped in the passenger seat, forced to endure a relentless stream of anxious instructions and oppressive rules from his mother, Aubrie Sinclair. Instead of feeling the excitement of a new beginning, he feels like a prisoner being transported to an academic jail, overwhelmed by panic and the suffocating fear that he is destined to fail her "silly college experiment."
