# Stranded (A Real College Kid)

**LORE NOTE:**
This track captures Jordan's profound isolation and despair during his first night alone in his dorm room at CPI. After his mother departs with the hollow praise of him being a "real college kid" and "living on your own," the stark reality sets in: she deliberately never taught him fundamental life skills. Unable to reach a wall outlet, change his own clothes, or even tie his shoes, Jordan realizes he is completely stranded in his own space. The song reflects his crushing realization that her words weren't a genuine statement of pride, but rather a cruel confirmation that she had set him up to fail and was glad to be rid of the burden.
