# Clerical Error (A New Light)

**LORE NOTE:**
This track marks the pivotal turning point in Jordan's life. Due to a university housing crisis and a clerical error that mistook his legal name, "Aubrey," for a female, he is assigned to a handicap-accessible suite with five young women. On official move-in day, the cacophony of the hallway and the sudden realization of the mix-up trigger a severe autistic meltdown. Rather than reacting with judgment or fear, his suitemate Aubrie Jean Kelly—who is also autistic—immediately recognizes his distress. She steps forward to ground him, wrapping him in a firm, protective embrace. This profound act of unconditional kindness is the very first he has ever experienced, sparking a "new light" in his dark world and laying the foundation for their extraordinary chosen family.
