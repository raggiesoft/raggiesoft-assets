# System.Crash(Meltdown_01)

**LORE NOTE:**
This track thrusts the listener directly into the chaotic sensory overload of Jordan's first major autistic meltdown in the story. Triggered by the deafening noise of freshman move-in day, the sudden presence of female strangers in his suite, and the terrifying realization of the housing mix-up over his legal name "Aubrey," his mind experiences a complete system failure. Unable to process the compounding stressors or execute an escape, he is paralyzed by panic, rocking in his wheelchair and covering his ears as his world temporarily shatters around him.
