# The Anchor Holds (Aubrie's Theme)

**LORE NOTE:**
This track shifts the perspective to Aubrie Jean Kelly during the chaotic events of move-in day. Being autistic herself, Aubrie instantly recognizes that Jordan's frantic rocking and covering of his ears is not an act of aggression, but a severe sensory and emotional overload. Gently explaining to her parents that he is simply overwhelmed, she steps into the room and takes control of the situation. By calmly asking for his consent and providing a firm, grounding embrace, she acts as a living anchor for his crashing system. This profound, unconditional act of care marks the very first genuine hug Jordan has ever received in his life, cementing Aubrie's role as his primary safe harbor.
