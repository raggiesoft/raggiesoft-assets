# The Same Constellations

**LORE NOTE:**
Following the chaotic events of move-in day and Jordan's subsequent meltdown, he and Aubrie share a quiet moment of connection. Jordan nervously reveals his assigned major, Astrophysics—a subject his mother deliberately chose as an insurmountable trap to ensure he would fail her "silly college experiment." He fully expects Aubrie to pity him or confirm his inadequacy. Instead, her face lights up as she joyfully declares, "Me too!" For Aubrie, the cosmos and the laws of physics are a logical, predictable sanctuary from a confusing world. Her genuine excitement shatters his mother's manipulative trap, transforming his greatest academic fear into a shared passion and a fragile, dawning hope for the future.
