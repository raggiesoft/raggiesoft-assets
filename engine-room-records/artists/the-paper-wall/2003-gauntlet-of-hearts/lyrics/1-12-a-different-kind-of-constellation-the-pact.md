# A Different Kind of Constellation (The Pact)

**LORE NOTE:**
This track details the radical and beautiful formation of the group's poly-affectionate dynamic. After Lauren is publicly humiliated and cruelly dumped by her ex-boyfriend, Jordan steps in to comfort her, offering her unconditional tenderness and a safe embrace. Watching this, Aubrie Jean realizes she feels absolutely no jealousy—only a profound sense of rightness and protective care. She proposes an unconventional solution: rather than competing for Jordan and treating him like a prize to be won, the single women of the suite (Aubrie, Madison, Zoe, and Lauren) agree to share his romantic affection as co-equals. Hannah, devoted to her boyfriend Mark, lovingly opts out of the romantic aspect but cements her role as his platonic best friend and official hug-giver. They cease to be roommates and become a "constellation" of support, fundamentally defining the open-hearted terms of Jordan's new life.
