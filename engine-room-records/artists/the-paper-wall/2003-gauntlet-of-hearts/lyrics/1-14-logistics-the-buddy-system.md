# [LOGISTICS] (The Buddy System)

**LORE NOTE:**
When Aubrie Sinclair cuts off Jordan's cellular service in a malicious attempt to isolate him, his chosen family immediately counters with a brilliant logistical defense. Zoe utilizes a VoIP app to secure a new, private phone number for Jordan that operates entirely over the university's WiFi, effectively bypassing his mother's financial control. To protect him when he is between buildings and off the network, Madison creates the "Jordan Comms" group chat and institutes a strict "Buddy System," ensuring he is never without a trusted family member by his side. Musically, this track is a turning point: it introduces bright, hopeful 80s synth-rock elements that actively push back against the dark, industrial tones of his past, symbolizing his new family's network successfully defeating his mother's oppressive control.
