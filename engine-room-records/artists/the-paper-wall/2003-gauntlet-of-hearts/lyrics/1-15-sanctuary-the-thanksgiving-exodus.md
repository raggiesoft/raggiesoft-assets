# Sanctuary (The Thanksgiving Exodus)

**LORE NOTE:**
When Aubrie Sinclair sends a Thanksgiving ultimatum demanding Jordan return to Hampton Roads, his chosen family orchestrates a swift escape. Utilizing the Maroon Express, the group travels to Christiansburg, Virginia, to seek refuge at the home of Zoe's family, the Brooks. Although it is just a short bus ride down the road, to Jordan, the journey feels like an exodus deep into the Appalachian mountains, far beyond his mother's oppressive grip. Upon arriving at the house on a hill, he is met with immediate, unconditional acceptance. Laura Brooks, Zoe's mother, doesn't see a burden or a broken child; she simply sees her daughter's friend—her "daughter's boy." For the first time in his life, Jordan experiences the warmth and safety of a true family home, cementing the Brooks' residence as his ultimate sanctuary.
