# Guardianship (The Summons)

**LORE NOTE:**
Back at CPI for the Spring semester, Jordan's hard-won peace is violently interrupted when he receives an ominous email summoning him alone to Hall Director Kelsey's office at 3:00 PM. Waiting inside is a Montgomery County Sheriff's Deputy. When the uniformed officer formally addresses him by his full legal name, "Aubrey Jordan Fuller," the trauma of his mother's abuse and the terrifying realization of her reach triggers a severe autistic meltdown. Kelsey expertly steps in to de-escalate the situation, but the damage is done. The envelope contains a court summons: Aubrie Sinclair has filed a petition in Norfolk to have Jordan declared legally incompetent and placed under her guardianship, launching a desperate, malicious legal war to drag him back under her control.
