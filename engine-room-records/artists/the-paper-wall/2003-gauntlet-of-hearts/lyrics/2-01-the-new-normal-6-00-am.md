# The New Normal (6:00 AM)

**LORE NOTE:**
Following their move to the Brooks' family home in Christiansburg, Zoe initially devises a highly efficient but physically punishing commute schedule. By putting the group on the 5:45 AM Maroon Express, they beat the morning rush, but are left waiting 45 minutes in the freezing cold before the CPI campus library opens. Recognizing the severe health risk this environmental exposure poses to Jordan's medically vulnerable system, Zoe abandons her initial plan and recalculates. She determines that the 6:00 AM Blue Ridge Transit Route 1 local bus is the optimal, safe alternative—trading a longer, 45-minute ride for a vastly reduced 15-minute wait in the cold. The song is a fast, analytical, and triumphant track representing Zoe's logical system rightfully prioritizing Jordan's well-being over pure efficiency.
