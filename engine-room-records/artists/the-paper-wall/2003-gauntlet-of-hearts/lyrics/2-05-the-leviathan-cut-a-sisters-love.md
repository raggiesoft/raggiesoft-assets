# The Leviathan Cut (A Sister's Love)

**LORE NOTE:**
As Jordan recovers from his fever, his chosen family decides to cheer him up with the ultimate movie marathon. Cult-following devotees Jack, Sarah, Molly, and Clare sit him down to watch the legendary, 12-hour, 21-minute "Leviathan Cut" of *The Silver Gauntlet of Aethel*. The film is an absolute 1980s cheesy fantasy adventure bliss. As the movie opens with its epic, synthesizer-and-guitar-driven anthem, "A Sister's Love (Main Theme)," Jordan is instantly captivated. The song, performed by the band Fireflight and featuring the powerful female lead vocals of Alannah Shaw, acts as a musical awakening for him. Watching the protagonist Kaelan embark on a perilous quest to save his twin sister Kaela, Jordan realizes he wants his own life's soundtrack to sound exactly like this: full of vibrant 1980s synthesizer power, driving rock energy, and unapologetic hope.
