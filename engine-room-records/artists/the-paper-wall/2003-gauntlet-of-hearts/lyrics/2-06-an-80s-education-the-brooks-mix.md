# An 80s Education (The Brooks Mix)

**LORE NOTE:**
After witnessing Jordan's sheer delight at the soundtrack of *The Silver Gauntlet of Aethel*, Daniel Brooks makes it his personal mission to guide "his daughter's boy" through the greatest musical decade. Realizing that Jordan's abusive upbringing denied him the joy of popular culture, Daniel curates the ultimate, definitive mixtape: "The Brooks Mix." This track is a joyous, high-energy explosion of gated snares, analog synthesizers, and stadium rock. It represents Jordan finally shedding the cold, oppressive industrial noise of his past and joyfully embracing the neon, unapologetic optimism of the 1980s.
