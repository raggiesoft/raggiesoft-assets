# Logically Unfounded (The Pact of Six)

**LORE NOTE:**
After confronting his deepest fears, Jordan still struggles with a lingering, analytical anxiety planted by his mother: the cynical idea that the unconditional love of his chosen family is "logically unfounded." Why would six people—Aubrie, Madison, Zoe, Lauren, Hannah, and Mark—dedicate themselves to his care, happiness, and protection without any ulterior motive or blood obligation? In response, the group gathers around him to formally declare "The Pact of Six." They dismantle his anxiety by explaining that love isn't a transactional equation to be balanced. Their commitment to him is not a temporary charity project; it is a permanent, foundational truth. The promise they make to him is absolute, undeniable, and crystal clear.
