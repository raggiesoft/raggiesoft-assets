**LORE NOTE:**
This track is unabashedly Cassidy’s favorite song on the album. It is a brilliant execution of pure, driving 1980s synth-pop, but transposed entirely into the deep cosmos. Had Apex Records allowed them to write music this imaginative in 1987, this would have easily been the lead single on their debut. Lyrically, it uses the astrophysical concept of a "tidal lock"—where a moon is gravitationally bound to always show the exact same face to its planet—as a gorgeous metaphor for unwavering loyalty. It also features a clever lyrical callback to the "anchor" motif from Disc 1, proving that their terrestrial past and their cosmic future are completely aligned.
