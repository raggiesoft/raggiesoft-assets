### **1-01-static.md**
***

**LORE NOTE:**
The opening statement of the "Freedom Era." Recorded in the warehouse with amps turned to 11, this track acts as a direct rejection of the "hollow sound" Apex forced the band to make for five years. The line "I am the static! I am the noise!" became the rallying cry for the "Ironhead" fanbase during the band's independent rebirth.

**LYRICS:**

(Verse 1)
You wanted pop, you wanted clean
A hollow sound from a thin machine
You tried to tune me out, you tried to change the dial
But I've been waiting all this while

(Chorus)
I am the static! I am the noise!
The signal breaking, the hidden voice
I am the static!
The feedback wail!
The story that you tried to derail!

(Verse 2)
This isn't synth-pop, this isn't grace
This is the sound of a different place
This is the heart you tried to hide
The part of us that never died

(Chorus)
I am the static! I am the noise!
The signal breaking, the hidden voice
I am the static!
The feedback wail!
The story that you tried to derail!

(Guitar Solo)

(Chorus)
I am the static! I am the noise!
The signal breaking, the hidden voice
I am the static!
The feedback wail!
The story that you tried to derail!

(Outro)
I am the static...
I am the static...
Can you hear me now?

### **1-02-garbage.md**
***

**LORE NOTE:**
Ryan's raw, "jangle-rock" rage track. It retells the infamous "dirty mirror" incident from the 1992 *Friction* photo shoot, where an executive tried to offer him cocaine on a mirror, and Ryan—innocently thinking it was dirt—wiped it clean with a napkin. The song channels his fury at the executive who humiliated him and tried to destroy his family.

**LYRICS:**

(Verse 1)
You brought your tray, your silver plate
You thought you'd open up the gate
You showed me powder on a glass
You thought you had a man of class

(Chorus)
It was just dust! It was just a lie!
So I wiped it clean, and I watched you die
You called it 'art', you called it 'style'
It was just garbage in a filthy pile!

(Verse 2)
You held your clipboard like a gun
You thought the battle was already won
You crossed the line, you showed your hand
You'll never, ever understand

(Chorus)
It was just dust! It was just a lie!
So I wiped it clean, and I watched you die
You called it 'art', you called it 'style'
It was just garbage in a filthy pile!

(Guitar Solo)

(Chorus)
It was just dust! It was just a lie!
So I wiped it clean, and I watched you die
You called it 'art', 'cuz you're a fake
It was just garbage, make no mistake!

### **1-04-atmosphere-warehouse-demo.md**
***

**LORE NOTE:**
The "Lost Gem" from the canceled 1992 *Friction* sessions. The band wrote this song about the literal, scientific friction of a space shuttle re-entering Earth's atmosphere ("Here comes the friction / Feel the fire"). The "rogue" executive, Julian Vance, misinterpreted these lyrics as sexual metaphors, leading to the catastrophic misunderstanding that destroyed the contract. This is the original 1992 demo, released here for the first time.

**LYRICS:**

This atmosphere... with you

***

**LORE NOTE:**
The epic closer of Side A. This arena-rock anthem is dedicated to the "Ironheads" and CPI alumni who funded the band's freedom through the "Forger Nation War Chest." The "Fortress" is both their warehouse studio and the protective wall their fans built around them.

**LYRICS:**

### **1-06-escape-velocity-ad-astra.md**
***

**LORE NOTE:**
The "Magnum Opus" that consumes the entirety of Side B. This 15-minute progressive rock suite is Cassidy O'Connell's autobiographical journey from the trauma of the trial ("The Cage") to the transcendence of artistic freedom ("The Cosmos").

Known by fans simply as **"Ad Astra,"** the track features a distinct "mechanical cutoff" sound between Part II and Part III, created by Ryan pulling the plug on his guitar amp to simulate a rocket's Main Engine Cutoff (MECO).

**LYRICS:**

[Part I: The Cage]
(Whispered, over a dark, shimmering synth pad)
The walls are cold, the air is thin
The noise outside is closing in
A single light, a buzzing sound
Another day in the underground

I'm running from the face I see
The "victim-saint" they made of me
The "wholesome girl," so sad and worn
A "David" for their Goliath's scorn

This rage is just a heavy stone
A gravity I've outgrown
Shedding skin, shedding weight
Escaping from this world of hate

They loved the trial, the "family" torn
They loved the story, a new star born
But not from music, not from grace
From the "hot-chocolate-drinking" "angel face"

The "Shark" was right, the "nuke" went off
We won the battle, but at what cost?
We're "radioactive," we're "litigious"
We're just the joke, the "victim-saints"

(Slightly louder)
I'm trapped inside this "Wilderness"
This "Fortress" built on bitterness
This isn't freedom, this is a cell
This "righteous hell"...
This "righteous hell"...

[Part II: Ignition (Ad Astra)]
(The dark synth pad is suddenly cut by a rapid, driving analog synth sequencer. A pounding, 80s acoustic drum beat kicks in, locked with a driving bassline. Vocals become strong, clear, and melodic.)

I see a signal in the endless black
A single light, no turning back
The pull of the old world fades away
A new horizon, a new day

The countdown's running in my head
Ignoring all the things they said
The "Engine" roars, a crimson light
A pillar of fire in the night

The pressure builds, I'm pinned to the seat
My heart is pounding with the beat
The G-force pulling, a heavy stone
The last chain breaks, I'm not alone

(Chorus)
I'm climbing! Climbing!
Past the point of no return
I'm flying! Flying!
With a fire I let burn
Just a signal, a distant light
Burning through the endless night
Ad astra... (To the stars!)
Ad astra!

(Instrumental Break)
(A short, fast, synthesizer and guitar riff-heavy section. Pure 80s prog-rock energy.)

The sky is tearing at the seams
Awake now from their broken dreams
The "Stardust" is just a trail of dust
The "Stardust" is the thing I trust

The clouds are falling far below
A place of pain I used to know
The blue gives way to endless black
There is no thought of turning back

(Chorus)
I'm climbing! Climbing!
Past the point of no return
I'm flying! Flying!
With a fire I let burn
Just a signal, a distant light
Burning through the endless night
Ad astra... (To the stars!)
Ad astra!

[Part III: The Cosmos]
(The driving beat cuts off abruptly. Tempo slows to a crawl. Vast, sweeping synth pads take over.)

I'm floating now, the engine's off
The silence is a holy sound
The rage is gone, the fear is lost
In this new universe I've found

The "Cage" is just a distant star
A tiny light I left behind
I finally know just who you are
The other half of my own mind

(Ethereal, layered vocals swell)
I'm loved by the sun
I'm held by the void
An orbit just begun
A life not destroyed

I'm loved by the moon
I'm cradled by the black
My heart is back in tune
There is no turning back

And the stars are singing
A song I understand
I'm finally, finally
Reaching for your hand

(Instrumental Bridge)
(Music swells. A long, beautiful, melodic, and highly emotional electric guitar solo. Full of hope, not rage.)

(Ethereal, layered vocals return)
I see the colors of the nebula
A place I've only seen in dreams
The reds, the blues, the purples call
Flowing in majestic streams

This is the place I was meant to be
This is the "Stardust" that is me
The cosmos and the rocket's fire
My one, my only true desire 
I'm loved by the sun

(Ethereal, layered vocalization)

[Part IV: Re-entry (The New World)]
(Music slowly fades back to just the shimmering synth pads and ethereal, whispered vocals)

The walls are gone
The air is clear
There is no noise
There is no fear

The "Stardust" is a forgotten ghost
The "Engine" has delivered me to the coast
Of a new world, a new shore
A place I've never been before

The "Cage" is broken, the "Victim-Saint" is dead
A new, clean script is in my head
The "Friction" burned away in the fire
Leaving only my true desire

(Ethereal, layered vocals swell for the finale)
I'm loved by the sun...
I'm held by the void...
My orbit has begun...
My life is not destroyed...

Ad astra...
Ad astra...
(To the stars...)

(Song fades out on a final, sustained, peaceful synthesizer chord)
