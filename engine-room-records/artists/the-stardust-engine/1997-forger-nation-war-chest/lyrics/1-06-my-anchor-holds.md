**LORE NOTE:**
Looking back from 1997, exactly ten years after its initial release, this track stands as the ultimate "Trojan Horse" of the band's Apex Records era. 

The studio executives heavily polished this power ballad and marketed it globally as a generic, sweeping romance to sell records. But for Ryan and Cassidy, the original studio cut was always a desperate love letter to their own unbreakable family bond. While they later evolved the song's meaning—most notably completely rewriting it as *My Anchor (The Sail)* on the *Hard Reset* album—this pristine 1987 recording captures the exact moment the siblings realized they were the only thing keeping each other sane under the suffocating pressure of the pop machine. It is a massive pop hit that secretly served as their survival anthem.
