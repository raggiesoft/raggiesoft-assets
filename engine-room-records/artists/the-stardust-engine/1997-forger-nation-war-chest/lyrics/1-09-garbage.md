**LORE NOTE:**
Continuing Act III ("The Wilderness"), *Garbage* is Ryan O'Connell’s unfiltered, jangle-rock middle finger to the corporate machine. The track immortalizes the infamous "dirty mirror" incident from the disastrous 1992 *Friction* photo shoot. 

During the shoot, an Apex Records executive attempted to offer Ryan cocaine on a silver-plated mirror. Ryan—who takes things quite literally—innocently assumed the mirror was just dirty and wiped the expensive powder away with a napkin. When he later realized he was being mocked, and that the executive was actively trying to drag his family into the industry's toxic underbelly, Ryan channeled his fury into this blistering, two-and-a-half-minute track. 

In 1995, singing these lyrics felt like a private vent session shared only with the hardcore "Ironheads" via mail-order cassette. Included here on this 1997 compilation, it stands as a globally broadcast, platinum-certified humiliation of the very executive who tried, and failed, to break the Engine.
