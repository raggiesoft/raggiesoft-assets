**LORE NOTE:**
Placed in the "Comeback" section of the compilation, *Echoes in the Wire* is the ultimate emotional payoff to the "Omission" note found in the album's liner notes. 

During the Apex years, Cassidy was forced to sing the romantic ballad *If You Walked Away*, which she experienced as a terrifying "what-if" scenario of her brother abandoning her. Upon gaining their independence, the band permanently retired that traumatic track. In its place, they wrote this pure, unapologetic 1980s power ballad. Instead of fear and abandonment, *Echoes in the Wire* is about surviving the storm together and leaving a legacy. It transformed a nightly moment of anxiety into a triumphant celebration of the family's unity, proving they didn't need a label to write a massive, stadium-shaking ballad.
