**LORE NOTE:**
Serving as the second movement of The Stardust Suite, this track bleeds directly out of the fading guitar chord of the album's opener. If track one reclaimed their past, *Hard Reset* is the explosive declaration of their "Freedom Era" future. This track also serves as the direct narrative sequel to their 1995 underground epic, *Escape Velocity (Ad Astra)*. Where *Ad Astra* ended with the ship clearing the atmosphere, *Hard Reset* begins with the terrifying silence of space. The main engines have cut out, and the crew realizes they must manually reboot the ship's core to stabilize their orbit. It is a brilliant metaphor for the band's own journey: they escaped the gravity of Apex Records, but now they must survive the vacuum of true independence.
