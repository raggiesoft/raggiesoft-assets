**LORE NOTE:**
Serving as the third and final movement of the epic "Stardust Suite," *Brand New Scene* resolves the tension built across the first two tracks. If Track 1 was the historical reclamation of their true sound, and Track 2 was the violent, terrifying manual override of their corporate constraints, Track 3 is the sound of an engine humming perfectly in the open vacuum. It is the moment of release. Ryan and Cassidy are no longer fighting Apex Records; they are just playing the music they were always meant to make. The bridge features a direct, triumphant lyrical callback to the bridge of *The Stardust Engine*, perfectly tying the 15-minute overture together.
