**LORE NOTE:**
After the massive, three-part narrative weight of the opening "Stardust Suite," the band shifts gears. If the first three tracks were about escaping the gravitational pull of Apex Records, *Static On The Dial* is about the freedom of the open road that follows. It is a quintessential, windows-down driving anthem. Notably, Verse 2 features a direct lyrical shoutout to the rhythm section (Evan on bass and Tyler on drums), cementing the fact that in the "Freedom Era," The Stardust Engine operates as a unified rock band, not just a pair of pop frontmen.
