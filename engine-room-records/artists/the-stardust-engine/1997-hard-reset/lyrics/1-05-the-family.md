**LORE NOTE:**
Originally conceived in 1987, this is the pure, unapologetic 1980s Rock and Roll anthem the band actually wanted to release on their debut. Instead, Apex Records forced them to shelve it in favor of the pop-leaning *Electric Color*. Ten years later, on *Hard Reset*, they finally recorded it exactly the way it was meant to sound. Driven by a massive gated-reverb snare and a relentless 130 BPM tempo, it serves as a joyous, upbeat tribute to their survival. Lyrically, the mention of "the five of us" officially canonizes their manager, Holly O'Connell, within the music itself—acknowledging that she is the architect of the very "fortress" that kept them together.
