**LORE NOTE:**
Driven by a massive, sludgy guitar riff that channels the heaviest, stomping arena-rock anthems of the 1970s, this track is pure sonic intimidation. Lyrically, Ryan and Cassidy take direct aim at the pristine, over-produced sound of their Apex Records days—"You muted the kick / You silenced the crash." By embracing this slow, thunderous tempo and dropping the pressure, the band is actively tearing apart their old pop-star uniforms and letting the heavy weather roll in.
