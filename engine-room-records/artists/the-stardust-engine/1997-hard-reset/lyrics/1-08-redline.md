**LORE NOTE:**
If *Thunderhead* was about dropping the pressure and bringing the slow, sludgy storm, *Redline* is the lightning strike. Pushing the tempo to a frantic, blistering pace, this track is the sonic equivalent of tearing the safety limiters off a massive engine. Lyrically, Cassidy and Ryan celebrate the dangerous, exhilarating speed of running their own label and making their own rules. There is no corporate oversight to tell them to slow down, so they are pushing the band to the absolute edge of control. It features one of the most technically demanding instrumental duels between Ryan's guitar and Cassidy's synthesizer in their entire catalog.
