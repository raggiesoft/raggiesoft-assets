**LORE NOTE:**
This track serves as the emotional climax and finale of Disc 1. It is a direct, intentional rewrite of their biggest 1987 pop hit, *My Anchor Holds*. While the original track was a desperate song about clinging to each other for survival under the crushing weight of their label, *My Anchor (The Sail)* flips the narrative entirely. Arranged as a massive, lighters-in-the-air 1980s power ballad, the lyrics shed the theme of codependency in favor of absolute equality. They are no longer holding each other down; they are a unified ship sailing into their "Freedom Era."
