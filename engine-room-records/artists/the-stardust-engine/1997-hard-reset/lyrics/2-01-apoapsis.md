**LORE NOTE:**
Opening Disc 2 ("The Twin Moons"), this track acts as the first movement of **The Apoapsis Suite**. Completely shedding the hard rock guitars of Disc 1, *Apoapsis* is a pure, 1980s ambient synth-rock instrumental. It represents the exact moment the Stardust Engine cuts its main thrusters and enters the silent, zero-gravity vacuum of deep space.
