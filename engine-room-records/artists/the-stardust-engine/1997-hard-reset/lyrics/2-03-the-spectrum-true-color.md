**LORE NOTE:**
The massive, 7-minute-and-54-second finale to **The Apoapsis Suite**. This mini prog-rock epic completely reimagines their 1987 corporate pop hit, *Electric Color*. Rather than a sanitized, terrestrial love song, the band transforms it into a sprawling observation of the cosmos. After more than ten minutes of pure instrumental atmosphere across the start of Disc 2, Cassidy's ethereal vocals finally drift into the mix at the 2:21 mark, anchoring one of the most ambitious arrangements of their entire career.
