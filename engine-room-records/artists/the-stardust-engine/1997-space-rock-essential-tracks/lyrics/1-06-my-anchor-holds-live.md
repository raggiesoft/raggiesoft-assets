**LORE NOTE:**
Act II of the compilation marks the band’s transition out of the studio and onto the stage. Recorded during their grueling 1989 *Neon Hearts Tour* (and released on the 1990 *Live in Chicago* album), this live cut of *My Anchor Holds* completely recontextualizes their 1987 pop hit.

In the studio, Apex Records had heavily polished the track and marketed it as a generic, romantic power ballad to sell records. But in the live arena, away from the immediate control of the executives, Ryan and Cassidy reclaimed it. When they sang this to each other on stage, it wasn't about romance; it was a desperate promise of survival. For the "Ironheads" in the audience, this performance was undeniable proof that the siblings were the only thing keeping each other sane under the suffocating pressure of the machine.
