**LORE NOTE:**
Act III of the compilation, "The Wilderness," opens with a violent tonal shift. After the sabotaged live recordings of the Apex era, the shrieking guitar feedback of *Static* immediately announces that the "Cold War" is over and the "Freedom Era" has begun. 

Originally recorded in 1995 inside the band's warehouse studio (with the amps literally turned to 11), this track was initially distributed underground. During those lean "Wilderness Years," the band dubbed cassettes and burned CD-Rs one at a time, mailing them directly to the "Ironheads" who kept them afloat. 

By 1997, following the massive commercial success of *Hard Reset*, Engine Room Records finally possessed the capital to professionally press and globally distribute *The Warehouse Tapes* to retail. Placing *Static* right in the middle of this "Greatest Hits" album is a victory lap. The rallying cry—"I am the static! I am the noise!"—is no longer an underground secret; it is a global broadcast.
