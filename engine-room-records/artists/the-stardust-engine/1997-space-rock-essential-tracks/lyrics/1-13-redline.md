**LORE NOTE:**
If *Thunderhead* was about dropping the pressure and bringing the slow, sludgy storm, *Redline* is the lightning strike. Pushing Act IV into absolute overdrive, this track is the sonic equivalent of tearing the safety limiters off a massive engine. 

For ten years under Apex Records, the band had "governors" placed on their sound to keep their tempo safe, clean, and radio-friendly. On this track, those limiters are violently ripped out. With their own label and zero corporate oversight to tell them to slow down, Cassidy and Ryan use this blistering tempo to celebrate the dangerous, exhilarating speed of making their own rules. It features one of the most technically demanding instrumental duels between Ryan's guitar and Cassidy's synthesizer in their entire catalog, proving that they are more than willing to push the band to the absolute edge of control.
