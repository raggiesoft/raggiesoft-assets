**LORE NOTE:**
Serving as the epic 6-minute finale to the core album, *Fade to Dawn* is proudly cited by both Ryan and Cassidy as their absolute favorite track on the *Midnight Drivers* project. 

Engineered in 2001 to act as the ultimate cathartic release for the "lost" 1987 record, this track masterfully bridges the two halves of the album. It takes the brooding, atmospheric Darkwave tension of Side B and slowly morphs it back into the triumphant, stadium-shaking Arena Rock of Side A. The song is a slow burn, opening with nearly a full minute of progressive instrumental tension before Cassidy's ethereal vocals even enter the mix. When Ryan's guitar finally shatters the darkwave atmosphere with a massive, wide-panned solo, it represents the sun finally breaking over the horizon. The band survived the night shift, and they made it through the dark together.
