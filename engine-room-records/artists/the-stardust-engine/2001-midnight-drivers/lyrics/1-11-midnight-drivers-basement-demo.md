**LORE NOTE:**
Included as an archival bonus track on the 2001 CD release, this is the absolute genesis of the *Midnight Drivers* concept. Recorded on a handheld cassette deck in 1986, this raw, unpolished demo features just Cassidy on an upright piano in their parents' basement while Ryan works out the vocal melody. 

To ensure strict compliance with modern digital distribution platforms and avoid automated metadata rejections, Engine Room Records officially cataloged the track simply as "Midnight Drivers (Basement Demo)," cleanly sidestepping any systemic confusion over its 1986 recording date versus its 2026 digital upload date. It strips away the heavy Darkwave production of the studio master, revealing the intimate, familial songwriting core that the band would ultimately build their empire upon.
