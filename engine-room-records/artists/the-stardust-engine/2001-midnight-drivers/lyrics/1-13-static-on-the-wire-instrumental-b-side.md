**LORE NOTE:**
To fully commit to the illusion of a lost 1987 record, Engine Room Records included this "Instrumental B-Side" on the 2001 CD release. In the 1980s, instrumental versions of hit singles were famously pressed on the flip sides of 7-inch 45 RPM vinyl records. By stripping away the vocals, this bonus track allows listeners to fully appreciate the meticulous, era-accurate synthesizer programming and drum machine loops the band engineered in 2001 to flawlessly recreate the sound of their corporate past.
