**LORE NOTE:**
Closing out the 2001 CD reissue is this raw, gritty instrumental outtake. To complete the illusion of the 1987 recording sessions, Engine Room Records included this "leaked" studio jam session featuring only the band's rhythm section: identical twins Evan (bass) and Tyler (drums).

According to the lore, Ryan and Cassidy had stepped out of the booth, and the twins locked the soundproof doors to keep everyone—including any hallucinated arena crowds—outside. With the tape left rolling, they laid down a blistering, unpolished groove. Devoid of any synthesizers or soaring vocals, this track puts the spotlight entirely on Tyler's heavy gated-reverb snare and Evan's driving, distorted bassline. It is pure, unfiltered rhythm section muscle, proving that even without the "Stardust," the engine still roars.
