

***
### **1-01-moon-2-roche-limit.md**
***

**LORE NOTE:**
Exploding out of the gate as the opening track for *Mile Marker 98*, this is the spark that ignited Ryan's 2003 solo orbit. Freed from the need to balance the "Stardust" with his "Engine," Ryan takes the astrophysical concept of the Roche limit—the exact point gravity tears a moon to pieces—and uses it to set the destructive, high-friction tone for the entire record. It serves as a violent, visceral thesis statement for his confrontation with the trauma of the "Crash of '90", introducing blistering, distorted electric guitars and pure, aggressive rock energy right from the first second.
