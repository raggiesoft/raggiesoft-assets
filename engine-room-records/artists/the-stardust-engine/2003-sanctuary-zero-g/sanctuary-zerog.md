### **1-01-moon-1-tidal-lock.md**
***

**LORE NOTE:**
As the opening track of her 2003 solo orbit, *Sanctuary (Zero-G)*, "Moon 1 (Tidal Lock)" expands from a single standout deep-cut into the foundational thesis of Cassidy's entire musical architecture. This is the ultimate, undiluted presentation of **The Cosmic Tidal Lock Sound™**. By kicking off the album with this 1980s Cosmic Pop masterpiece, she establishes the zero-gravity environment for the rest of the record—a sanctuary built on unwavering loyalty, sweeping spatial reverb, and mathematically perfect analog synthesizers. It is the sound of the "Anchor" finding her perfect, peaceful center of gravity.
