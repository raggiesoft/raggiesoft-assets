**# 📘 **Rowlands Family: Old Names vs. New Names**

## **Mother**

|Old Name|New Name|Notes|
|---|---|---|
|**Ellen Hayes**|**Eira Rowlands**|“Ellen” was the placeholder before you established her Welsh identity. “Eira” (snow) reflects her Anglesey roots. “Rowlands” is her reclaimed maiden name after the divorce.|

## **Father**

|Old Name|New Name|Notes|
|---|---|---|
|**Tom Cross**|**Thomas Cross**|No name change — but “Thomas” is his formal name, and the source of Julian’s middle name.|

## **Children (Before the Julian Abduction Arc)**

All three children originally carry **Tom’s surname (Cross)**.

### **Gwendolyn**

|Old Name|New Name|Notes|
|---|---|---|
|**Gwendolyn Cross**|**Gwendolyn Arwen Rowlands**|First name stays. Middle name added: _Arwen_ (Welsh + LOTR resonance). Surname changes at age 18 by her own choice.|

### **Gabriel**

|Old Name|New Name|Notes|
|---|---|---|
|**Gabriel Cross**|**Gabriel Idris Rowlands**|First name stays. Middle name added: _Idris_ (fiery lord; Welsh mountain). Surname changes at age 18 by his own choice.|

### **Julian**

|Old Name|New Name|Notes|
|---|---|---|
|**Julian Cross**|**Julian Thomas Rowlands**|First name stays (named after Julian Lennon). Middle name “Thomas” inherited from Tom. Surname changed by court order after Tom’s death.|
