# Part 2: The Confrontation

It was a Saturday afternoon when a tentative knock came at the door of Room 214. Madison opened it to find a young woman with long brown hair and kind, sad eyes. She recognized her instantly from Mark’s description. It was Sarah.

"Hi," Sarah said softly, her eyes scanning the room and landing on Lauren. "I'm... I'm Sarah. Can I please talk to you, Lauren?"

The entire suite went on alert. Lauren tensed, and Zoe immediately stood up, ready to intervene. "I'm not here to fight," Sarah said quickly, sensing their protective wall. "I promise. I'm not mad at you."

Lauren looked at her friends, then back at Sarah’s genuine, pain-filled expression, and nodded slowly. "Okay."
