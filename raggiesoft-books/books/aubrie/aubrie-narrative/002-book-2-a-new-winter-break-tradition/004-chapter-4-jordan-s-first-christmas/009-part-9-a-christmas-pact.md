# Part 9: A Christmas Pact

After the glorious feast, the entire Group of 17 returned to the basement, full and happy. The "Human Elevator" of Zoe's brothers helped Jordan back downstairs, and Aubrie carried his chair. They all settled in to resume watching *Holiday House-Sitter 2: Lost in the Big City,* unpausing it right as the main character began launching bricks at the burglars from a rooftop.

Jordan was on the large sectional sofa, with Aubrie snuggled up on one side of him and Madison on the other, his arms wrapped protectively around the two women he had shared Room 214 with. The lights from the Christmas tree cast a warm glow, and outside, the world was quiet and still.

While the rest of the family was roaring with laughter at the elaborate booby traps on screen, Madison, ever observant, was the first to notice that the quiet, happy glow on Jordan's and Aubrie's faces hadn't faded since their private talk earlier.

"Okay, you two, spill," she whispered, nudging Jordan playfully. "There's something I need to know ahead of us returning to Room 214 in a couple of weeks, isn't there?"

Aubrie's blush, which had never fully faded, grew even deeper. She leaned closer to Madison, as if spilling a big secret. "We kissed," she whispered excitedly.

Madison put two and two together instantly. This was more than the quick, friendly pecks she herself had shared with Jordan. She’d seen the way they looked at each other after they came out of that room. She giggled, her own cheeks turning a little pink. "Oh, you *kissed* kissed," she whispered back, realizing that Aubrie had shared a proper, first-time romantic kiss with him.

There wasn't a hint of jealousy in her heart; she was just incredibly happy for them both. Aubrie, seeing the genuine joy on her roommate's face, felt a wave of relief and excitement.

"This changes nothing," Aubrie whispered hurriedly, wanting to be clear. "We'll still have our sleep schedule. You... you can still kiss him, too. All of you can. If you want." She looked at Jordan, then back at Madison. "He's not a prize to be won. He deserves all the love he can get. He's not a burden."

Jordan, listening to this, pulled both women a little closer, his heart full. Madison noticed that Aubrie's face was glowing even more now, a small, determined smirk on her lips. She sensed there was something else.

"What else is on your mind?" Madison prompted gently.

Suddenly, Aubrie looked very nervous. She knew exactly what was on her mind, but it wasn't something you just said out loud.

Madison giggled softly, understanding. She leaned in and whispered, "You're ready to have sex with Jordan, aren't you."

It wasn't a tease; it was a statement of fact, delivered with complete understanding. Aubrie blushed to the roots of her hair, which Madison took as a definitive "yes." She knew Jordan's history, how sheltered he had been, and that there were things about being an adult he still needed to learn in a safe, loving environment.

Aubrie finally found her voice, a weak whisper. "I am... I'm ready for that with him. With my boyfriend." She was afraid Madison might tease her, but Madison just gave her hand a reassuring squeeze.

"You know," Madison whispered, "we both really like him in that way. All of us do, except Hannah out of respect for Mark, and that's totally fine. But for the rest of us... you know, we could... if you're comfortable..." Madison struggled to finish the thought.

Aubrie, finding a sudden surge of courage, completed it for her. "Yes," she whispered, turning to include Jordan in their huddle. "We could be in the same room 214 together." She looked directly at Jordan, her eyes full of love and an incredible offer. "We both could... be with you. When you're ready."

It was Jordan's turn to blush. He remembered her words from earlier: *"We can do whatever you want to do."* He suddenly understood the full meaning. He thought of the lessons from HSC 201, about consent and communication. He trusted these two women more than anyone in the world.

"If we ever have a quiet place," he whispered, his voice thick with emotion, "with just the two of you... I'd like to try. With both of you. Aubrie first, please."

"Of course," Aubrie and Madison whispered in unison. "We'll talk about this again," Aubrie promised. "We both promise."

They turned their attention back to the movie just as the kid on screen slipped on the ice and was finally caught by the bad guys.

When the movie finally ended, it was time for bed. Jordan was completely overwhelmed by the entire day—his first real Christmas, his first truly passionate kiss, and now, the promise of a future intimacy he had never thought possible. He was exhausted and wanted to go straight to bed. And he really wanted both Aubrie and Madison with him.

They both understood. They took him to the bathroom to help him get cleaned up and changed into his pajamas. Behind the closed door, they both quietly reiterated their promise.

"When we get back to the dorm, and it's just the three of us," Aubrie murmured, "we can. If you still want to." "And tonight," Madison added, "the bed is going to be a tight fit with three of us, but we'll make it work. How about you sleep in the middle, so you can hug both of us?"

He nodded, a look of pure, trusting gratitude on his face.

Once they were all in their pajamas, they headed back to the bedroom, where Madison announced the change in plans. "Hey guys, Aubrie and I are both sharing with Jordan tonight."

Zoe, practical as ever, immediately offered a solution to the lopsided numbers. "Okay. I'll sleep alone on this mattress then. No problem."

Soon, they all took to their beds. Jordan was in the middle of a queen-sized portable bed, Aubrie on one side, Madison on the other, both snuggled in close. He was exhausted, happy, and very much looking forward to returning to CPI when the Spring semester resumed.
