# Part 3: A Successful Shopping Trip

After lunch, they shopped. Aubrie, seeing Jordan was still a bit uncomfortable in his chair, used one of her gift cards to buy him a thick, high-quality memory foam cushion from a specialty store. She immediately swapped it out for his old one.

"There," she said, as he settled into it. "Does that feel better?" A sigh of pure relief escaped him. "Yes," he said, a genuine smile spreading across his face. "Why yes, it feels so much better!"

The others used their gift cards to buy small things for each other. While they browsed, Madison took a moment to walk with her brother Bob and her cousin Emily. The two cousins were communicating quietly between themselves using the soft clicks and coos of their Heart-Hum.

"So, how are you really liking it here?" Madison asked Emily gently.

Emily smiled, a real, happy smile that reached her eyes. She pulled out their shared mPad and quickly typed a message for Madison to read.

The screen showed: So much better, Madi. I'm so much happier here. I think... I think I want to go to CPI when I turn 18.

Madison beamed, thrilled for her cousin's newfound peace and ambition. She pulled Emily into another hug.

Finally, their successful shopping trip came to an end. They said their goodbyes to the Scotts and headed back to the bus stop.
