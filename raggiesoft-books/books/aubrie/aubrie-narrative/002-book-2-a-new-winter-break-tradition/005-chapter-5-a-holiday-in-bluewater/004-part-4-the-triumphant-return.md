# Part 4: The Triumphant Return

The group of seven boarded the 1:15 PM Orange Line. The local bus wound its way back through town, arriving at the downtown Bluewater Transit Center at 2:45 PM, just in time to transfer to the waiting Maroon Express.

Jordan Comms: \[LOGISTICS\] Zoe signing on for the group. All 7 of us are on the 2:45 PM Maroon Express from Bluewater. Destination: Falling Branch. ETA 3:45 PM.

An hour later, they were walking the final, familiar block from the park and ride back to the Brooks' house. Jordan felt a profound sense of accomplishment. He had shopped at an indoor shopping mall during the busy holiday season, and this time, he hadn't gotten overwhelmed. He hadn't needed a rescue. He had done it.
