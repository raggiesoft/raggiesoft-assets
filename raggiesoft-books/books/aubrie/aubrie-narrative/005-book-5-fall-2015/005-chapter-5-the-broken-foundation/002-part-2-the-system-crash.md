# Part 2: The System Crash

Aubrie, sitting beside him, felt Jordan let go of her hand and saw him clutch his chest. "J? What's wrong?" she whispered, her eyes wide with alarm.

"I... I don't feel good," Jordan managed to say, his voice strained. "Chest..."

Madison, on his other side, leaned in, her own face pale. "Is it a panic attack?"

A few seats down the row, Mark had been watching the professor, but the small, frantic motion of Jordan's hands caught his eye. He saw Jordan rub his chest, then saw his hand move up to grip his jaw. Mark's blood ran cold. He’d seen this before. It wasn't a panic attack. It was what his uncle had looked like right before he collapsed.

*No...* Mark thought, his mind racing. *He's 19. It can't be. But... the sweat, the chest, the jaw...* He didn't wait. He scrambled past Hannah and Sarah, moving down the row to get a clearer look.

"Jordan? Jordan, look at me." Mark knelt in the aisle, his voice sharp and low. Jordan's eyes were glassy, his skin a pale, clammy gray.

Mark's disbelief vanished, replaced by sheer, agonizing certainty. *It is. It's a heart attack*.

"Zoe, call 911! NOW!" Mark's shout cut through the murmur of the lecture.

The Group of 8 snapped to attention. Aubrie and Madison were frozen in terror. Mark didn't wait for Zoe to dial; he bolted from the row, sprinting down the auditorium steps toward the lectern, frantically waving his arms.

"Professor!" he yelled, his voice cracking with panic. "Jordan's having a heart attack! We need 911! We need help!"

The professor, stunned by the interruption, saw the sheer terror on Mark's face and the commotion in the back row. He didn't hesitate. He grabbed the university phone at the lectern and dialed campus emergency services. "Yes, this is SOC 101 in Walton Hall! I have a student collapsed, possible heart attack! Send paramedics immediately!"

Back in the row, the lecture hall now deathly silent, Zoe's fingers were flying on her mPhone. She hit the macro for the Jordan Comms chat.

Jordan Comms:

Zoe: \[URGENT\] \[MEDICAL\] JORDAN COLLAPSING IN SOC 101. CHEST PAIN. MARK THINKS HEART ATTACK. 911 CALLED. \[11-13-2015 09:32:14\]

The message blasted out to Daniel and Laura, who were at their respective jobs and couldn't immediately see it.

Mark ran back up the stairs, his heart hammering. He looked at his friend, who was slumped forward, conscious but barely. *He's 19 years old,* Mark thought, his mind screaming. *He's too young for this*.

The professor's voice boomed over the suddenly silent hall. "Help is on the way. The paramedics are en route".
