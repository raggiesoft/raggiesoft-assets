# Part 7: The Shower Protocol

The evening wound down, and the happy chaos of the group’s return settled into a sleepy, domestic peace. It was time for Jordan to get ready for bed.

"Alright, J," Hannah said, moving toward the bathroom. "Time to get you washed up."

"Actually," Aubrie said, cutting her off with a warm, pointed smile. She looked across the room at Megan, who was cleaning up the last of the dinner dishes. "Megan, you were so incredible with him last night, and you're so strong. Would you mind taking the lead on this tonight?"

Megan looked up, feeling the eyes of the entire pact on her. She knew this was more than a request for help; it was an invitation. It was the pact of five, seeing her internal conflict, and actively giving her permission. Her heart hammered, but she nodded, trying to look casual. "Of course. No problem at all."

She wheeled Jordan into the accessible basement bathroom. "Alright, transfer protocol," she said, positioning his chair next to the shower bench. "Ready?"

In one surprisingly strong and fluid motion, Megan lifted Jordan from his chair and settled him securely on the bench. It was a move that usually required both Hannah and Lauren to coordinate, but Megan managed it safely on her own.

Zoe, who had followed them in with the discharge binder, made a mental note. *New protocol: Megan is the primary agent for all high-risk transfers.*

Megan began the clinical act of care, following the strict rules from the binder. She meticulously kept the incision on his chest dry while she carefully washed his hair and body. Her mind was a whirl. *This is just care. It's medical. He's my brother. He's Zoe's family.* But as she performed the task with a gentle, respectful hand, she felt that now-familiar, dangerous flutter in her stomach. She was deeply happy to be trusted this much, but she immediately reinforced her internal boundary. *This is a sister's job. This is not romantic.*

Jordan, for his part, was acutely aware of her. He could sense her conflicted energy. He knew she liked him; he'd felt it since the night of his meltdown. But he was afraid to say anything, terrified of breaking the "sister" boundary she had established. He decided to test the waters, just a little.

"You know," he said, his voice casual, "when Aubrie or Madi help me with this, they usually just hop in and take their own shower after. Since they're already in the bathroom and all".

Megan's hands froze on his shoulder. A deep, hot blush flooded her entire body. She was completely flustered. The invitation was implied, but the 8-year age gap screamed in her head. She was unable to answer.

"Oh, I... I..." she stammered, immediately defaulting to her caregiver role. "Okay, let's get you rinsed off."

She quickly finished washing him, helped him get dried, and into his pajamas. She was quiet, her mind racing. She wheeled him back out into the main room, where the rest of the Group of 8 was waiting.

They knew something was changing. The energy between Jordan and Megan was palpable and awkward. Jordan looked embarrassed, worried he'd pushed too hard. Megan looked like she'd been caught.

Zoe, the analyst, processed the data: 1. Megan is conflicted. 2. Jordan is interested. 3. The pact is supportive. 4. The only barrier is Megan's internal script.

Zoe decided to apply a logical override. She walked over to her sister.

"Megan," Zoe said, her voice clear and kind, "you're welcome to explore your feelings".

Megan's blush, which had just started to fade, returned with a vengeance. She was now completely exposed, but also, for the first time, given explicit, logical *permission*.
