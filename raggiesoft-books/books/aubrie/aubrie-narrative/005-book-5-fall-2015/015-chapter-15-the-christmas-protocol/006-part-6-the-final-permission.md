# Part 6: The Final Permission

Megan's heart was hammering. Jordan was still holding her hand, his eyes shining with a love that was no longer hesitant. She looked at the five other women, expecting... she didn't know what. A flicker of jealousy? A hint of buyer's remorse?

She saw nothing but joy.

"Well," Madison said, breaking the silence, her voice full of a playful grin. "That was a good start. A little... polite, but good."

Megan blushed, confused. "What... what do you mean?"

"She means," Aubrie said, her smile so warm it melted the last of Megan's anxiety, "that you're still acting like you're in trouble. You're not. You're one of us now. You're allowed to *really* kiss him."

"We're not just 'okay' with it, Megan," Lauren added gently. "We're *happy* about it. We've been watching you two dance around this for weeks. It was getting painful."

Megan looked at Jordan, who was watching her with an expression of pure adoration. He was her safe person. He was the one who had made her feel wanted again. And this entire family was giving her their blessing.

"Go on," Madison encouraged, her voice soft. "Kiss him like you mean it."

Jordan, his confidence bolstered by the full support of his pact, didn't wait. He leaned in again, and this time, Megan met him with a new certainty. This kiss wasn't hesitant. It was a deep, passionate, and explicitly romantic kiss, a full and loving exploration.

When they finally broke for air, Megan was breathless. She looked up at the pact of five, her mind braced for the inevitable sting of jealousy that all her life experience told her *must* be there.

But there was none. Aubrie was beaming. Zoe was giving her a small, satisfied nod. Madison, Lauren, and Sarah were all smiling, their faces full of nothing but pure, uncomplicated happiness for their two friends.

Megan couldn't believe it. She had just tongue-kissed Jordan, passionately, right in front of his five other girlfriends, and they were *happy* about it.

That was the final piece. The last of her social conditioning, her fear of the age gap, her anxiety about "stealing" him, all of it evaporated. This was real. This was the pact. And she was, finally, a part of it.

Her mind immediately went to the rule Zoe and Sarah had mentioned. The practical part. The responsible part. She, like them, was now in a sexual relationship with Jordan, even if it was currently on a medical hold.

Okay, she thought, her mind clear for the first time in weeks. Okay. I'll call my OB/GYN on Monday. I need to make an appointment to get on birth control. I'm part of this now, and I'm going to be responsible about it.
