# Part 2: The 10:00 AM Rush

It was Friday morning. The air in the Brooks' basement was thick with the same intensity as a launch control room. The dining table had been transformed into a command center. Three laptops were open, screens glowing.

At **9:50 AM sharp**, the "virtual waiting room" for the CPI Events portal opened.

"Go, go, go!" Zoe commanded.

Jordan, Mark, and Zoe all hit the login button simultaneously. They typed in their student credentials, their fingers flying. A moment later, all three screens refreshed to show a spinning maroon circle and the message: **"YOU ARE IN LINE. DO NOT REFRESH. TICKET SALES BEGIN AT 10:00 AM."**

"We're in the chute," Mark said, leaning back but keeping his hand hovering near the mouse.

The next ten minutes were an agony of waiting. 9:55 AM came and went.

"Status check," Zoe called out, her eyes fixed on the atomic clock on her tablet.

"Cell Alpha, ready," Jordan reported. He was sitting in his wheelchair, his mouse hovering. His heart was hammering against his ribs. Beside him, Aubrie and Madison were watching his screen like hawks. Megan, the guest of honor for his cell, stood behind him, her hand resting supportively on his shoulder.

"Cell Beta, standing by," Mark said from the other end of the table. He had Hannah sitting next to him. His mission was to secure tickets for himself, Hannah, and Daniel and Laura.

"Cell Gamma, green," Zoe confirmed. She was buying for herself, Lauren, and Sarah.

"Remember the protocol," Zoe instructed, her voice calm but sharp. "We are targeting **Section 104, Row A**. That is the accessible field-level row. Jordan needs the wheel-in spot, and we need the companion seats directly adjacent."

9:59:30 AM.

"Hands off the keyboards," Zoe commanded. "Do not refresh. The page will auto-update via AJAX. Wait for the signal."

They watched the seconds tick down on the atomic clock. 57... 58... 59...

10:00:00 AM.

On all three screens simultaneously, the gray "COMING SOON" button flashed and instantly turned a vibrant, electric green: **"BUY TICKETS."**

"Go!" Zoe shouted.

"I'm in!" Jordan yelled, clicking the button the millisecond it changed. The seating chart loaded instantly. He scanned the field map and saw the blue wheelchair icon for Section 104. "I see it! Row A, Seat 1!"

"Grab it!" Megan cheered.

Jordan selected the wheelchair space and the three companion seats next to it. "Cell Alpha locked."

"Cell Beta locked," Mark called out seconds later.

"Cell Gamma locked," Zoe finished.

They all hit "Next." But instead of the payment screen, a new page appeared: **"EXCLUSIVE FORGER NATION ADD-ONS."**

Jordan scanned the options.

**1. Ceremonial Ticket Stub (\$5.00)** *High-quality, gold-foil souvenir printed on heavy cardstock. Featuring custom art of The Iron Forger.* **WARNING:** SOUVENIR ONLY. NOT VALID FOR ENTRY. POLY PASS REQUIRED. NO EXCEPTIONS.

**2. The Soundboard Direct: Live at The Crucible (\$15.00)** A raw, unmixed audio CD-R burned directly from the soundboard feed. Available for pickup at The Foundry 24 hours after the concert. Note: This is a raw archival recording to hold you over until the official mixed album release in the Fall.

"Guys, look!" Jordan said, pointing at the screen. "We can get physical stubs! And a CD of the show right after it happens!"

"Get them," Aubrie said instantly. "We need the stubs for the memory box. And you *definitely* need that CD."

Jordan grinned and clicked "Add to Order" for four stubs and one copy of the Soundboard Direct CD-R.

"Cell Alpha checking out," he announced.

"Same here," Mark said. "Getting stubs for everyone."

They hit "Complete Purchase." The screens spun for a terrifying second... processing... processing...

"CONFIRMED. ORDER COMPLETE."

The room erupted. Madison shrieked and hugged Aubrie. Mark high-fived Hannah. Jordan slumped back in his chair, letting out a massive breath of relief as the digital confirmation appeared on his screen.

The Stardust Engine: Live at The Crucible Section 104, Row A. Ticket Holder: Jordan Shawn Kelly Access Method: Poly Pass (Ending in 8842) Add-Ons: (4) Ceremonial Stubs, (1) Soundboard Direct CD-R

He looked up at Megan, beaming. "We got 'em. We're going to the front row. And we're taking the music home with us."
