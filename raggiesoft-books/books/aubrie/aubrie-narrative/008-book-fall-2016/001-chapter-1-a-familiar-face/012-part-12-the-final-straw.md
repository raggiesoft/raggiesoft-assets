# Part 12: The Final Straw

On Tuesday afternoon, the Group of 8 walked Jessica back to Forge Hall. The plan was for her to quickly grab her books and a change of clothes before heading to the transit center with them. Jordan, Aubrie, and Madison accompanied her up to her room while the rest of the group waited in the lobby.

"I'll just be a second," Jessica said, fumbling for her Poly Pass.

She unlocked her door and pushed it open. The three of them froze on the threshold.

The room was dark, the curtains drawn, but it was not empty. Loud, thumping bass music was still playing, as it always was. And in the dim light, they could clearly see that both of her roommates were in their respective beds, and neither was alone. They were actively engaged with their boyfriends, completely oblivious to the door opening. The room was a mess, clothes strewn everywhere, and the air felt thick and stale.

Jessica let out a small, strangled sound, a mixture of disgust and humiliation. This was her *home*. Her *space*. And it had been completely **violated**.

"Oh, my god," Madison whispered, her face a mask of disgust.

Jessica immediately slammed the door shut, her entire body shaking. "I... I can't," she whispered, leaning against the wall in the hallway. "I'm not going back in there. I **never want to come back** to this room. Ever".

Aubrie, her face a mask of cold fury, put her arm around Jessica's shoulders. "You're not," she said, her voice an absolute promise. She pulled out her mPhone. "You're moving out. Tonight."

She immediately sent a message to the Jordan Comms chat.

**Aubrie:** \[URGENT\] \[LOGISTICS\] Change of plans. Jessica is moving out. Now. Her roommates are disgusting pigs. We're at Forge Hall. Daniel, Laura, we need a car. We're getting her things.
