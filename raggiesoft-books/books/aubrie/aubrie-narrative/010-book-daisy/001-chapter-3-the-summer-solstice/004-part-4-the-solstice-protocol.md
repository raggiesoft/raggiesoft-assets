# Part 4: The Solstice Protocol

A few days later, Mark and Hannah brought Daisy home to the Compound. The family gathered on the back patio, keeping a safe distance at first due to the pandemic protocols, just watching the baby sleep in her bassinet.

"June 20th," Zoe mused, checking the date. "She really waited for the Solstice."

"The day with the most light," Aubrie said, smiling at Jordan. "It fits. After everything... the darkness of the surgery, the pandemic... she came with the light."

Jordan looked at his wives—Aubrie, Madison, Zoe, Lauren, Sarah, Megan, and Jessica. He looked at the baby. An idea formed—a logical, beautiful system for their future.

"We should aim for this," Jordan said. "When we start... for us. We should try to have them on the Solstice. Like Daisy."

Zoe’s mind ran the calculations immediately. "To achieve a mid-June birth date, conception must occur within a specific window in late September or early October of the previous year. It allows for efficient batching of resources and shared birthday celebrations."

"Super Birthdays," Madison grinned. "One massive party every few years. All the cousins together."

"The Solstice Protocol," Sarah agreed.

It was decided. The **Procreation Pact** now had a schedule. They would wait, helping raise Daisy and surviving the pandemic, and then, they would aim for the **Solstice of 2024** to begin the first wave of the Kelly children.
