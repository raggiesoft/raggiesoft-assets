# Part 5: The Unwinding

Back in the sanctuary of the Brooks' basement, the Group of 8 was utterly exhausted. The long day of travel and social interaction had taken its toll, and a single thought was on everyone's mind.

"I have never needed a shower more in my entire life," Madison declared, speaking for the whole room.

What followed was the practiced, efficient chaos of eight people navigating a single bathroom. There was no awkwardness, just a shared mission to get clean and comfortable. When it was Jordan's turn, Hannah and Lauren stepped up.

"We've got you," Hannah said simply. Mark, who was grabbing water bottles for everyone from the small kitchenette, just gave her a supportive smile and a nod. He was immensely proud of the kind, compassionate woman he loved, and he trusted her completely.

After everyone was clean, they all began changing into pajamas. This was Jordan's moment. With the group watching with quiet encouragement, he began the process of dressing himself. He successfully pulled his pajama shirt on over his head, a look of intense concentration on his face. Then, he moved to the new technique for his pants. He positioned his chair next to one of the portable beds, braced his arms, and with a grunt of effort, lifted his hips just enough to pull his underwear and pajama pants up to them.

It was a huge accomplishment, but he couldn't get them the rest of the way. He looked up at his friends, not with defeat, but with a look that simply asked for the final assist.

"I'll lift," Hannah said immediately, moving to support his weight. "I'll pull," Aubrie added, quickly and smoothly pulling the waistband up to his waist.

A small, spontaneous round of applause broke out from the others. "You did it, Jordan!" Madison cheered. "That was amazing! You did most of it yourself!" He beamed, a proud, tired smile on his face.

As they all prepared for bed, Madison, usually so bubbly, was noticeably quieter. The revelations about her aunt and the emotional weight of her conversation with Emily had left her feeling unnerved. She needed comfort tonight. Without a word, she went to the bed that had been designated for Jordan and sat on the edge. The group understood immediately.

The pairings for the night settled into place. Mark and Hannah cuddled up on one queen bed. Aubrie and Zoe took another, with Lauren and Sarah taking the third. Finally, Jordan transferred into his bed, and Madison slid in beside him, grateful for the quiet, unconditional comfort.

The lights were turned off, and a chorus of weary "goodnights" filled the room. Exhausted from their long day of adventures, the eight of them drifted off to sleep, safe and together.
