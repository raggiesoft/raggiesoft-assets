# Part 8: The Promise of the Night

As the group finished their meals in the food court, a new, unspoken energy settled over them. Everyone could see the looks of quiet, intense longing on the faces of Madison and Aubrie, and the happy, trusting expression on Jordan's. They knew it was time to get the three love birds back to the Old Lady.

They exited the mall and walked to the New River Mall Christiansburg Transit Center, arriving at 7:23 PM. As if on cue, they saw the familiar headlights of an RVT Roanoke Express coach pulling into a bay. Its destination sign cycled between BLACKSBURG-CPI, FALLING BRANCH, and via INTERSTATE 81.

"That's the last one tonight," Zoe noted.

They boarded the bus, Jordan using the long-haul coach's mechanical lift. With everyone on, the bus departed ahead of schedule at 7:25 PM for the quick ride back to campus. They then headed straight to Bennett Hall.

When they got back to the suite, Mark and Hannah immediately took their leave. "We're gonna head out for the night," Mark said with a warm, knowing smile. "See you all in the morning."

This left Jordan alone in Room 214 with the five women of his pact. The air was thick with a new, beautiful anticipation. Following the rules of their new pact, they all began to get ready for the night, changing into their comfortable birthday suits. Bed A looked very inviting. In the bed with Madison, Jordan found himself kissing her. Their tongues slowly started to explore. The four other women (Zoe, Lauren, Sarah, and especially Aubrie) looked on with happy blushes. The man that they considered part of this found family was finding happiness as he made out with Madison. None of them felt any pang of jealousy, not even Aubrie. The making out soon found itself turning into something more, and the four women stayed right there just in case anything went wrong with what was only Jordan’s third time exploring this new level of intimacy (and first time with someone other than Aubrie).

Later, after a long, intimate, and deeply trusting exploration with both Aubrie and then Madison, a happy and contented exhaustion settled over the three of them. The other ladies of the pact saw that Jordan was perfectly happy, and that their protective presence wasn’t needed anymore. They got dressed and made their way through the bathroom to Room 216. Lauren and Zoe got themselves ready for bed, and Sarah exited the suite through Room 216. Once the connecting bathroom was available: First Aubrie, and then Madison, made a quick trip to the adjoining bathroom. When they returned, they helped Jordan with his own needs. They all decided to stay in their birthday suits, comfortable in the complete trust and privacy of their inner sanctum.

It was Madison's turn to join Jordan in his bed tonight. As she slid under the covers, he did something new. He turned and became the big spoon, wrapping his arm protectively around her. “When Summer Break starts, I want to join you and Aubrie on one of the queen beds. Maybe we can even be in our own room? We’ll see what happens,” Madison said, as she adjusted Jordan’s arm so that it wasn’t pressing into her hip (which was starting to get a little painful). Aubrie offered Jordan one last romantic kiss goodnight, letting her tongue explore Jordan’s tongue. Both women blushed, because there was no jealousy in this moment. He was no longer afraid of the dark. She then went to the bunk beds and slid into Bed B (the lower bunk, the bed she was officially assigned to) and pulled the blankets over. If this was how she was going to spend the rest of her life, she was a very happy woman. Now that she was across the room in Bed B, Aubrie smiled, her heart full of a pure, uncomplicated joy at seeing the two people she loved most find comfort with each other.
