# Part 1: The 7 AM Stalemate

The door *clicked* open at 7:00 AM, precisely on schedule.

Zoe was already awake. She had given up on sleep around 4:00 AM, her logical mind defeated by the relentless, inefficient two-hour interruptions. She was stiff, exhausted, and incredibly grumpy. Her respect for Aubrie and Lauren, who had endured this before her, crystallized.

ZOE.Systems.Logs.KernelLog: This is an unsustainable long-term protocol. 11-18-2015 07:00:02

"Good morning," the day nurse said, flipping on the lights. "How are we feeling?"

"Tired," Jordan rasped. He, too, was used to the interruptions by now, but he hated them.

As the nurse took his vitals, she glanced out the window at the swirling vortex of white. "Well, would you look at that," she said, surprised. "That weather system wasn't supposed to last this long. Looks like it just stalled right over the valley". "You two are officially snowed-in for a while".

The nurse finished her checks. "Vitals are looking good, Jordan. You're stable."

"Am I still NPO?" Jordan asked immediately, his voice desperate. "I am *so* hungry".

The nurse smiled as she checked his chart. "Good news. Dr. Hassan updated your chart overnight. You tolerated the clear liquids well. We're advancing you to a 'Soft Cardiac' diet. Low sodium, low fat. I can order you some oatmeal and maybe some scrambled eggs?"

Jordan let out a breath of pure, profound relief. "Yes. Please. Both. Anything."

Zoe felt a corresponding wave of relief. If he could eat, she could eat.

ZOE.Systems.Logs.SystemSchedulerLog: Jordan.Dietary.Protocol updated: 'Soft.Cardiac'. 11-18-2015 07:05:10

ZOE.Systems.Logs.ActionLog: 'Protocol: Self.Nourishment' is now unblocked. 11-18-2015 07:05:11

"Okay, Jordan," Zoe said, standing up and stretching her stiff muscles. "While they get your breakfast, I'm going to get dressed and grab my own".

She grabbed her clothes and her bag and, remembering the new protocol they'd established, headed for the privacy of the en-suite bathroom to change.

ZOE.Systems.Logs.ActionLog: Executing 'Protocol.Change(EnSuite)'. Reason: 'External_Observer(Hospital_Staff)' risk == High. 11-18-2015 07:06:01

She emerged a minute later, dressed and ready. "I'll be right back," she promised. "I'll get my food to-go. We'll eat together."
