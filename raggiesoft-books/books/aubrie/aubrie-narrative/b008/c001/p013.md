# Part 13: The Extraction

The group's response was instantaneous. Zoe, Mark, and the others came up from the lobby. Within fifteen minutes, **Daniel and Laura Brooks arrived** in the minivan, Daniel's face set with a grim determination.

This time, Mark and Daniel led the charge. They didn't knock. Mark threw the door open, flooding the room with light from the hallway.

The occupants of the beds scrambled, shouting in protest.

"Everyone out," Daniel's voice boomed, carrying the full authority of a father. "Now. You have sixty seconds to become decent and leave the room. My daughter's friends are moving her things."

The roommates and their boyfriends, terrified and embarrassed, grabbed their clothes and fled into the hallway, muttering curses.

The Group of 8 descended on the room like a practiced extraction team. Hannah and Lauren grabbed her bedding, Zoe and Sarah packed her desk, while the others grabbed her clothes. They had all of her possessions packed and loaded into the minivan in under ten minutes.

As they walked away from Forge Hall for the last time, Jessica looked at the chaotic, loud, and disrespectful dorm. She then looked at Jordan, waiting for her by the van, his hand outstretched. She knew, with absolute certainty, that she would rather have the puppy pile and its serene calmness than the chaos of her roommates. She had found her real home.
