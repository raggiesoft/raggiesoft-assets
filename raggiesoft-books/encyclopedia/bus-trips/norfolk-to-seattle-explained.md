# Norfolk → Seattle Pathfinder Trip (April 2006)
## Detailed Day-by-Day Timeline

**Estimated Total Duration:** ~4 days  
**Structure:** 3 nights in transit → arrival on Day 4  
**Travel Mode:** Pathfinder Intercity Bus (Greyhound equivalent)

---

# Day 1 — Departure and Virginia Corridor
## (Norfolk → Richmond → Roanoke → Knoxville)

### Morning — Departure Phase
- **Norfolk, VA — Departure**
  - Arrival via CVTA local buses:
    - Route 85 → Route 47 → Route 45 → Route 2
  - Pathfinder terminal located ~1 block from Route 2 stop

- **Richmond, VA — Bus Change**
  - First major transfer hub
  - Still within familiar territory

### Midday — Western Virginia
- **Charlottesville, VA — Scheduled Stop**
- **Staunton, VA — Rest Stop**
- **Roanoke, VA — Rest Stop**

### Afternoon / Evening — Appalachian Corridor
- **Marion, VA — Scheduled Stop**
  - Marion Ridge Training Center (FVAP)

- **Wytheville, VA — Rest Stop**
- **Bristol, VA → Bristol, TN — Border Transition**
- **Bristol, TN — Terminal Stop**

### Nightfall — First Major Transfer
- **Knoxville, TN — Bus Change**

---

## Narrative State — Day 1
- Energy: Alert → mildly fatigued  
- Emotional tone: Committed but not yet overwhelmed  
- Physical state: Stiffness begins, no real pain yet
